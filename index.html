Privacy Policy

App Name: Be Perfect!
Package: com.beperfectapp.android
Last updated: June 19, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Be Perfect! handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Data We Collect

Be Perfect! collects only the data you directly enter into the app:

  • Habits: Name, category, icon, reminder time, frequency, and completion status.
  • Habit Logs: Completion timestamps and skip records. Logs are automatically deleted after 90 days.
  • App Preferences: Theme selection and onboarding status.

All of this data is stored locally on your device only. It is never uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties.

2. Third-Party Services

Be Perfect! optionally uses the following third-party services to enhance your experience:

  • Anthropic Claude API — When you view a habit reminder, the app may send the habit name and category to Anthropic's API to generate a short motivational message (e.g., "Keep going on your meditation habit!"). No personally identifiable information is sent. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Unsplash API — When you add a new habit, the app may request a relevant photo using search keywords generated from your habit's category. No personally identifiable information is sent. See Unsplash's Privacy Policy.

Both services are optional. If the API calls fail or no API key is configured, the app falls back to built-in content and works fully offline.

3. Notifications

If you enable reminders, Be Perfect! schedules local notifications on your device. These are processed entirely on-device and no notification data is sent to any server.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information.
  • We do not use any analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not share or sell any data to third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

Be Perfect! is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
